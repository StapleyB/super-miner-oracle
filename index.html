Helpful Information:

Because you will never roll another Egg once you already have 4, any predicted Super Miner will be influenced by the number of Eggs you have. All predicted Super Miners will thus have attached the required number of Eggs you need to have in your inventory at the time the chest is opened.

If you want a Super Miner in the <4 eggs category, but you have 4 eggs you are currently leveling, you have two options: scrap one before opening (you'll find another in due time), or wait for the desired Super Miner to show up in the 4 eggs category.

If you want a Super Miner in the 4 eggs category, but you do not currently have 4 eggs, you will have to hope you will get 4 eggs by the target chest. Unfortunately, this page does not yet have implemented a tool to check how many eggs you will get by a target chest number.
